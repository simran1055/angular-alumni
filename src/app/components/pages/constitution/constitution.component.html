    Member type and eligibility for membership to the association is categorized as defined below:

    2. Alumni Member:Any person who has received Ph.D, Master degree, degree, diploma, certificate ICD conferred by the SLIET, Longowal, shall be eligible to enroll as. Alumni Member.
    3. Faculty and Technical Staff Member:The academic and technical staff, and registrar of the SLIET, Longowal, other than those falling under category i.e, 2.1 ( above shall be eligible to become Staff Member 8.
    4. Honorary Member:SAA may decide to confer honoraray membership to eminent persons.
  8. Enrollment:The rules for enrollment of members to the association are as follows:
    1. Alumni Members:All individuals who have been conferred Ph.D, Master degree, degree, diploma or certificate by SLIET, Longowal and whose membership fees have been received by SLIET Alumni Association, will be automatically deemed to be a member of the alumni association. Alumni members are expected to register with the alumni association for future communication and periodically update their contact information in the alumni database, online at the SAA website.
    2. Staff Members:The academic and technical staff including visiting academic staff and the Registrar, who are not alumni of SLIET shall be entitled for registration of membership in the form prescribed by the executive committee of the association
    3. Honorary Members:Eminent persons may be admitted to be the honorary members, as may be considered by the executive committee of the association, in consultation with the patron. Ex- Patrons of SAA automatically become Honorary Members after they cease to be the Director of SLIET, Longowal
  9. Alumni Registry:A registry and/or an electronic database of members shall be kept at the registered office of the association.
  10. Changes of Address Notification:If a member changes his/her postal and/or e-mail address, he/she shall notify his/her new address to the Alumni Association Office, and the entry in the registry shall be accordingly changed; but if he/she fails to notify his/her new address, the address in the roll of members or Registry shall be deemed to be his/her address.
  11. Alumni-SAA Communication:All communication to the members will be through e-mail, except where the member makes a request in writing for alternative means of communication.
