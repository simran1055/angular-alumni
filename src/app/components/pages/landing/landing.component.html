Welcome to SLIET Alumni Assosiation (SAA)

Founded in 2017, SAA is registered under Societies Registration Act (XXI of 1860) and as amended by Punjab Amendment Act, 1957. The primary mission of SAA is to make fruitful gap between Alumni and its Alma Mater along with Students of SLIET. We hope you have a great experience visiting our website and to learn to about various facilities, benefits and services you get to enjoy as a registered member.
